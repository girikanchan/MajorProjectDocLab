    cardiovascular disease (CVD) risk assessment tool that is used to estimate an individual's 10-year risk of developing atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease (ASCVD). It's named after the Framingham Heart Study, a long-term cardiovascular study conducted in Framingham, Massachusetts.

  • Gail Model (Breast Cancer Risk)

    Gail Risk Assessment Tool or Gail Model, is a widely used breast cancer risk assessment tool that helps estimate a woman's risk of developing breast cancer over a specified period.
